Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

Information Collection and Use

The Excel Versioning Add-in does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data or information. All operations performed by this add-in occur entirely locally within your Excel application.

Data Storage

This add-in does not store any data on external servers. Any versioning or snapshot data created by the add-in remains within your Excel workbook and is never transmitted outside of your device.

Third-Party Services

This add-in does not communicate with any third-party services or external APIs. It operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.
